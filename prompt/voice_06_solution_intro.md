# VOICE Section Rubric: Solution Introduction
Target duration and words:
- 10 seconds
- 20-25 words

Must include:
1. Product name
2. What it does
3. Differentiator

Rubric (0-5):
- Word Count: 20-25
- Clarity: one sentence makes the solution clear
- Differentiation: explicit contrast vs alternatives

Penalties:
- 30 words: -1
- Missing differentiator: -1
